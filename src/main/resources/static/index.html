Unitech Engg Works is a leading supplier and processor of graphite raw materials for industrial applications. Our expertise spans high-temperature machining, electrode manufacturing, and gascut component supply.

Located at the heart of Chennai's industrial corridor, we combine decades of materials expertise with a commitment to on-time delivery and stringent quality control.

  • Specialised in natural and synthetic graphite raw materials
  • Industrial gas-cut components for furnace and metallurgical use
  • Custom machining and processing to specification
  • Direct supply from verified, high-grade raw material sources
  • Serving metallurgical, chemical, and manufacturing sectors
What We Offer

Products &
Materials

High-grade graphite materials and precision gascut components for every industrial application.

01 ——
🔷

Graphite Electrodes

High-purity graphite electrodes for electric arc furnaces (EAF) and ladle furnaces. Available in UHP, HP, and RP grades.

02 ——
🧱

Graphite Blocks & Bricks

Industrial-grade graphite blocks for lining, thermal insulation, and high-temperature structural applications.

03 ——
⚙️

Gas-Cut Components

Precision gascut parts for metallurgical furnaces — nozzles, bushings, crucibles, and custom-profile cut components.

04 ——
🪨

Graphite Powder & Flakes

05 ——

CNC-machined custom graphite components to exact customer drawings — fast turnaround with tight tolerances.

06 ——
🏭

Refractory Graphite

High-thermal-conductivity refractory graphite for continuous casting, induction furnaces, and chemical reactors.
